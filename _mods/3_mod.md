Auto Zebra Trading automatically manages your gold by trading with zebras when gold reaches your specified threshold. This mod builds on the Automation Panel infrastructure.

## Configuration

When the mod is running in the Automation panel:

1. **Gold Threshold %**: Enter the gold cap percentage at which trades should begin (default: 99%)
2. **Season Toggles**: Check/uncheck seasons where trading should be enabled
3. **Start/Stop**: Toggle the automation on and off with the button

## Status Messages

The mod displays real-time status including:
- Current season and whether it's active
- Gold percentage and how long until threshold is met
- Trade execution details (amount traded, gold spent, gold remaining)
- Resource constraints (catpower/slab limitations)
